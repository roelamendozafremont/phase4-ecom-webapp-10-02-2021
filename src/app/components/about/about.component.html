Kitchen Story is an e-commerce portal that lets people shop basic food items on their website.

The website needs to have the following features:



A search form in the home page to allow entry of the food items to be purchased by the customer.

Based on item details entered, it will show available food items with price.


Once a person selects an item to purchase, they will be redirected to the list of available items. In the next page,

they are shown the complete breakout of the order and details of the payment to be made in the payment gateway. When payment is done,

they are shown a confirmation page with details of the order.!
